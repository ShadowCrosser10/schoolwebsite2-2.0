Home

NCSD, A Commitment to Excellence. The school's immersive STEM programs, enriching lessons and textbooks, are all reasons why I adore my school. NCSD consistently is ranked one of the top 20 schools in Michigan for over a decade. The first school was built in 1977, but prior to that, students were taught from buildings in neighboring towns. This website will allow you to learn more about the school, programs, opportunities and achievments.

The Library

The NCSD Elementary School library is a great place, where you can find large collections of informative, nonfiction, fictional, and books. The librarians are always honest and help you find whatever you are looking for. Enroll at NCSD to have more of these experiences and gain access to thousands of books, dvd's recordings, and audiobooks.

Robotics Lab

Robotics is an interactive program that enhances kids STEM skills. You get a chance to build a real-world robot that can make an impact in society. Not only that, but you get to PROGRAM the robot as well, giving you a glimpse of professional robotics. Attend NCSD for more of these amazing experiences!

Statistics

54% of Middle and High School students in NCSD are invlolved in some athletic program, including soccer, basketball, volleyball, and football teams.

29% of students are involved in musical arts like orchestra, band, choir and theatrical arts like drama and dance.

22 AP (Advanced Placement) courses and 15 IB (International Baccalaureate) are offered at NCSD, including history, math, computer science, etc.

NCSD is a 1 for 1 learning environment. Students in Kindergarten and 1st Grade are offered an ipad to access certain learning tools, research and read e-books on, and take standardized tests. kids in grades 2-12 are offered a chromebook to learn with.

75 different languages and dialects are spoken in NCSD, like Hindi, Japanese, and Spanish, making us a diverse community.

The graduation rate of high school students in the 2018-19 school year was 96%, far above the state median of 82%.

185 clubs, STEM programs, and sports teams are available to join, some of which are the brainchild of enthusiastic students and volunteers.
